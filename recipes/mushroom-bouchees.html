Mushroom Bouchees

Description

Great mushroom appetizers for friends and family gatherings!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 can mushrooms (drained)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ½ cup half-and-half
  • Salt and ground pepper
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 10 slices sandwich bread

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 175 degrees Celcisu (350 degrees fahrenheit)
  2. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray
  3. Chop green onions and mushrooms really finely
  4. Melt butter in a saute pan over medium heat
  5. Stir in green onions and mushrooms
  6. Cook until they are fragrant
  7. Whish cornstarch into half-and-half
  8. Pour mixture into saute pan and cook; stirring often until it thickens (approx. 3 minutes)
  9. Season with salt, ground pepper and garlic powerder
  10. Roll the bread slices flat and thin
  11. Spread the mushroom mixture onto bread
  12. Tightly roll the bread slices
  13. Slice each roll into 3 centimeter (around 1 inch) rounds
  14. Place rounds onto a baking sheet
  15. Bake until bread browns, about 10 minutes

There it is! Delicious and crunchy mushroom bouchees!
