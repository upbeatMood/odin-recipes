Chile Cheese Triangle Toast

Description

An easy to make appetizer for parties!

It is easy, simple and quick!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup shredded cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons diced jalapeno
  • 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
  • 6 slices of potato bread

Steps

  1. Preheat the over to 200 degress Celcius (400 degrees Fahrenheit)
  2. Mix mayonnaise, cheese, butter, jalapeno and hot sauce in a bowl
  3. Cut potato breads into 4 triangles
  4. Spread the mix on each slice
  5. Place the slices on a baking sheet
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 minutes until light brown (approx. 5 min)

Now they are ready to eat! Bon appetit!
